Aadhaar Biometric Update Policy Intelligence Dashboard

This dashboard presents a data-driven analysis of Aadhaar biometric update patterns across India. It helps policymakers identify demand hotspots, age-driven trends, and regional inequalities to improve Aadhaar service delivery.
